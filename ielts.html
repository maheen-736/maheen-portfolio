IELTS Exam Structure

Understanding the IELTS format is the first step to success. Here's what you need to know:

Listening

30 minutes to answer 40 questions testing your ability to understand spoken English in various contexts.

Reading

60 minutes to complete 3 reading passages with 40 questions testing reading comprehension.

Writing

60 minutes to complete 2 writing tasks (report/letter and essay) demonstrating your written English skills.

Speaking

11-14 minute face-to-face interview assessing your spoken English through conversation and discussion.
